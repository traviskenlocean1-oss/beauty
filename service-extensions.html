Questions

Frequently Asked

When installed and maintained correctly by a trained specialist, hand-tied and tape-in extensions cause minimal to no damage to your natural hair. The key is proper installation tension, regular maintenance appointments, and correct at-home care. We will never install extensions that we believe could compromise your hair's health.

Brush from ends to roots using a soft-bristle extension brush, wash 2–3x per week maximum with sulfate-free shampoo, and never sleep with wet hair. Avoid applying heavy oils or conditioners near the bonds or tape. We provide a full aftercare kit and guide with every installation service.

Yes — our Remy human hair can be colored. However, we strongly recommend having the color service done in our studio by one of our color specialists who are experienced with extension hair. Lightening is possible but requires care, and we'll advise on any limitations based on your specific hair's condition.

Hand-tied installations take approximately 3–4 hours for a full head. Tape-in installations take 1.5–2 hours. We recommend blocking a full half-day for your first appointment. Move-up appointments are significantly shorter — typically 1.5–2 hours for hand-tied, 45–60 minutes for tape-in.

Hand-tied extensions use wefts sewn onto tiny beaded rows — they're flatter, more natural-looking, and ideal for fine or thin hair. Tape-in extensions use adhesive tape sandwiched around your natural hair — faster to install, easier to maintain, and excellent for adding length and volume quickly. Your consultation will help determine which suits your hair goals and lifestyle best.
