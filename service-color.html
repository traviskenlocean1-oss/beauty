The Experience

How Your Color Day Unfolds

01

Color Consultation

We review your hair history, desired result, lifestyle, and porosity. Reference photos are welcome and encouraged — they help us calibrate the exact tone.

02

Custom Formula

Every color is mixed to order for your specific base, undertone, and desired result. No two formulas are the same, and yours is saved for future visits.

03

Application & Processing

Meticulous application using the technique best suited to your goal. You'll be under our signature steam treatment during processing time for maximum penetration.

04

Tone, Finish & Care Plan

A toner is applied to perfect the result. We finish with a glossing treatment, full blowout, and a personalized at-home care regimen to protect your color investment.

Questions

Frequently Asked

A full balayage service typically takes 3–4 hours, depending on hair length and density. We include the consultation, application, processing, toning, and full blowout finish. We recommend arriving with clean, dry hair for the most accurate placement.

For standard color services (highlights, root touch-up, single process), you can book directly. For color correction, vivid colors, or significant transformations, we strongly recommend a consultation first — either in-person or via our virtual consult option — so we can accurately quote time and price.

We'll build you a personalized home care protocol at every color appointment. In general: use a sulfate-free shampoo, a color-depositing conditioner matched to your tone, weekly deep conditioning, and always use a UV protectant before sun exposure. We carry all recommended products in-studio.

Every color service at Solène is paired with a bond-protecting treatment (Olaplex or equivalent) built into the formula. We're obsessed with the integrity of your hair — we will never push a service your hair isn't ready for. If your hair needs a repair plan before color, we'll tell you honestly.

That depends entirely on your starting point and target result. A 2–3 level lift is often achievable in one session with minimal compromise. Significant lightening (4+ levels, dark-to-platinum) almost always requires a phased approach to protect your hair's integrity. We'll be fully transparent in your consultation about what's achievable and what timeline looks like.
