Meet the Team

The Artists Behind
Every Transformation

We're a collective of passionate hair artists who believe that truly great hair begins with listening — deeply understanding your lifestyle, your vision, and your hair's unique personality.

01

Isabelle Moreau

Creative Director & Color Specialist

Isabelle founded Solène with a singular vision: to bring the editorial hair culture of Paris to the warmth of Miami. Trained at the Vidal Sassoon Academy and with over 12 years at the forefront of the industry, she is Miami's most sought-after colorist — known for her breathtaking balayage, impossible-to-replicate blondes, and the kind of color that looks better at week eight than it did on day one.

Specialties
Balayage Blonde Mastery Vivid Color Color Correction
12 Years Experience 2,400+ Clients Served
Book with Isabelle
02

Sofia Reyes

Extensions Expert & Texture Artist

Sofia is Miami's leading extensions specialist and a certified master of texture. Whether your hair is naturally curly, coily, or somewhere beautifully in between — Sofia understands it. Her hand-tied extension work is flawless, her braiding and natural hair styling is unmatched, and her dedication to protecting the integrity of your natural hair throughout every service is unwavering. She books months in advance for good reason.

Specialties
Hand-Tied Extensions Tape-In Extensions Curly Hair Texture Work
9 Years Experience 1,800+ Clients Served
Book with Sofia
03

Jade Laurent

Senior Stylist & Treatment Specialist

Jade is the studio's resident hair health obsessive — a senior stylist whose diagnostic eye for hair integrity has saved countless clients from the damage spiral. She is Solène's go-to for keratin smoothing, Olaplex bond building, and the kind of precision cut that holds its shape through 8 weeks of growth. Jade's approach is deeply consultative — she will always tell you what your hair needs, not just what you ask for.

Specialties
Keratin Treatments Bond Building Precision Cuts Hair Repair
7 Years Experience 1,200+ Clients Served
Book with Jade
04

Aria Chen

Bridal & Event Stylist

Aria brings a fashion-forward sensibility to every style she creates — from editorial blowouts that could walk a runway to wedding updos that photograph at every angle. With a background in high fashion editorial and a portfolio that spans bridal magazines and music video sets, Aria translates haute couture hair into real-world wearability. She is the studio's creative soul and the reason brides fly in from New York.

Specialties
Bridal Styling Event Updos Editorial Blowouts
6 Years Experience 900+ Clients Served
Book with Aria
