The Philosophy

A Great Cut Is the Foundation of Everything

At Solène Studio, no two cuts are the same. Before we pick up a single pair of shears, we sit with you — we learn how your hair grows, how you style it each morning, what shape feels like you. Then we create a cut so well-calibrated to your life that it looks effortless six weeks later, not just on the day.

How It Works

Your Cut Experience

01

The Consultation

We discuss your lifestyle, how much time you spend styling, your hair history, and what you've loved or hated about past cuts. This is never rushed.
